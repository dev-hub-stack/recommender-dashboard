# Available Recommendations - Quick Explanation for Client

## What It Shows

**"Available Recommendations" = Number of products we can recommend to this customer**

---

## Simple Example

### Customer: Adil

**What Adil bought:**
- MASTER FOAM
- GOLD PILLOW
- MOLTY FOAM

**Similar customers (107 people) also bought:**
- Everything Adil bought, PLUS
- MASTER ALPHA
- SLEEP WELL PILLOW
- COMFORT MATTRESS
- ... 20 more products

**Result: 23 Available Recommendations**

These are 23 products Adil hasn't bought yet, but similar customers have.

---

## How It's Calculated

```
Step 1: Find customers who bought similar products to this customer
Step 2: Look at what THEY bought
Step 3: Remove products this customer already owns
Step 4: Count the remaining unique products
```

**Formula:**
```
Available Recommendations = 
  Products bought by similar customers 
  MINUS 
  Products this customer already owns
```
